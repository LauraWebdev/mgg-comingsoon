What is MyGarage.games?

MyGarage.games will be a free platform to discover and share creations made in Nintendo's Game Builder Garage.

You'll be able to create your own profile and share all your games and creations with people all around the globe. Each game has its own dedicated page where you can add screenshots and trailers and receive feedback through the comments section. We want to make sharing AND discovering of Game Builder Garage content to be as easy as possible!

Who is creating MyGarage.games?

Hey, my name is Laura and I'm a 23 year old developer from Germany and the lead developer. I'm the lead developer behind SpinShare, a custom content sharing website for the rhythm game Spin Rhythm XD and along with a team of dedicated developers, I've created MakerWorlds, which was the very first open platform to share and bundle up Super Mario Maker when it came out in 2015.

Along with a small team of content moderators, I've decided to announce MyGarage.games early to gather initial support for such a platform.

When will MyGarage.games be available?

Our platform will open up on the games official launch date, June 11th 2021.

You can catch up on the latest development news on our Discord where we'll share development updates and announce development streams on Twitch.

How can I help? Where can I get updates?

Let others know! We aim to reach as many future players of the game as possible, so giving us a shoutout is the easiest way of supporting us.

We aim to make MyGarage.games open-source, so once we've launched, you'll be able to adjust and improve the code on GitHub. Check out our Discord for more information.

MyGarage.games is a free platform by the community and in no way affiliated with Nintendo. Game Builder Garage is a trademark by Nintendo.
